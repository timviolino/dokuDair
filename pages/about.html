About DokuDair

It all started with speedsudoku.com. I love sudoku, but what makes it more challenging and fun is the ability to race against others in real time. Along with offering a new and updated version of live sudoku competition, this was also a project and progression into the vast CS world. So we decided to learn and invite the community to play on and connect with the world. We hope you enjoy!
